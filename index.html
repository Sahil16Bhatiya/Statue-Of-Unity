Statue Of Unity

Kevadia, Gujarat 393155

The Statue of Unity is the world's tallest statue, with a height of 182 metres, located near Kevadia in the state of Gujarat, India. It depicts Indian statesman and independence activist Vallabhbhai Patel, who was the first deputy prime minister and home minister of independent India and an adherent of Mahatma Gandhi.

Statue of Unity

statueofunity.in

The aromatic view of hills surrounding the Statue of Unity will help you to reduce your stress. Moreover, the statue faces the Sarovar dam, and it provides a relaxing landscape and mesmerising sound. The architecture here is meritorious, and the statue has an exhibition hall, viewing gallery, and significant lift inside to enhance the tourist experience. You can also get an aerial view of the monument with the helicopter ride of 10 min. Tickets are available on the site or can be booked online. The price per head is Rs. 2900.

Jungle Safari Park

narmadatentcity.info

Located just 2 km away from the Statue of Unity, this is the perfect place to visit with your family. It has more than 1500 Birds and Animals and almost a total of 62 species of them. These species include fauna from Africa, Asia, Australia, and America. This park also has India’s largest ‘Walkway bird dome’ through which you can see numerous birds. There is an entry fee, which is ₹ 125 for kids and ₹ 200 for adults. It will be started for trial basis from 18th February 2020 and with the help of public reviews, further improvements will be done..

Sardar Sarovar Dam

Navagam, Kevadia, Narmada District, Gujarat

Situated 3.8kms from the Statue of unity; it is a gravity dam on the Narmada river, that supplies four Indian states, i.e. Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Maharastra and Rajasthan. Regarded as one of the largest dams in the world, it is a good place for outdoor activities, like hiking, sighting birds and crocodiles and exploring the mountain ranges.
